\paragraph{Remark (projected complex carrier).}
After the frozen complex structure $J$ is fixed, coefficient-wise complexification promotes the octonion carrier to complex $8$-tuples.
In \texttt{HQIV\_LEAN} this layer is Mathlib's Hermitian model \texttt{EuclideanSpace\,\mathbb{C}\,(\mathrm{Fin}\,8)} (\texttt{WeakComplexOctonionCarrier} in \path{Hqiv/Algebra/WeakInComplexStructure.lean}), with \texttt{WithLp.ofLp}/\texttt{WithLp.toLp} mediating bare coefficient vectors \texttt{Fin\,8\,\to\,\mathbb{C}}.
The Hermitian pairing \texttt{weakCarrierCinner} is the explicit slotwise sum, and \texttt{weakCarrierCinner\_eq\_inner} identifies it with Mathlib's \texttt{inner} on \texttt{EuclideanSpace\,\mathbb{C}\,(\mathrm{Fin}\,8)} (so orthonormality lemmas apply after rewriting).
The electroweak bookkeeping plane \texttt{weakJComplexDoublet} carries the standard Pauli half-generators; \path{Hqiv/Physics/WeakDoubletCarrierGaugeQuadratic.lean} packages the real $2\times2$ Gram matrix \texttt{weakWPlaneGramMatrix\,g\,v} for the $(\sigma^1,\sigma^2)$ directions on the VEV ray.
At the outer-horizon gauge data $(g,v)=(\texttt{su2CouplingDerived},\texttt{vacuumExpectationValueGauge})$ it is a scalar multiple of the identity, hence both eigenvalues equal \texttt{weakWPlaneGramEigenvalue\,g\,v}; in particular \texttt{weakWPlaneGramMatrix\_trace} gives trace $=2\lambda$ and \texttt{weakWPlaneGramMatrix\_det} gives $\det=\lambda^2$ for that common eigenvalue $\lambda$.
The bridge \texttt{M\_W\_derived\^{}2\,=\,8\,*\,weakWPlaneGramEigenvalue\,\dots} matches the factor-$8$ packaging used in the main manuscript, and \texttt{ew\_carrier\_gram\_mass\_certificate} chains this spectral data directly to the rational witnesses \texttt{boson\_witness\_M\_W} / \texttt{boson\_witness\_m\_H} in \path{Hqiv/Physics/DerivedGaugeAndLeptonSector.lean}.
The same file adds a carrier quartic \texttt{higgsQuarticPotentialCarrier} built from \texttt{higgsCarrierNormSq} (the squared carrier norm) and the geometric gauge vev anchor, with \texttt{m\_H\_sq\_eq\_two\_lambda\_times\_vgauge\_sq} implementing the $m_H^2\propto \lambda v_{\mathrm{gauge}}^2$ normalization with \texttt{higgsQuarticLambdaGaugeWitness} defined from existing masses (no new tunable parameter).
Triality generation tags \texttt{So8RepIndex} connect to the harmonic ladder through \texttt{weakPhiOfShellOnSo8Rep} and the closed-form $\alpha^{-1}$ readout \texttt{weakOneOverAlphaEMAtRep} (aligned with \texttt{one\_over\_alpha\_EM\_derived} in \path{Hqiv/Physics/SM_GR_Unification.lean}).
For machine-readable PDG comparisons, rational \texttt{\#eval} mirrors and small \texttt{example} bounds appear at the end of \path{Hqiv/Physics/WeakDoubletCarrierGaugeQuadratic.lean}.
The narrative anchor in the long-form paper is the ``Projected complex carrier'' paragraph in \path{papers/paper/octonion_lightcone_to_oshoracle.tex}; the electroweak witness ladder is summarized in \path{papers/hqiv_lean_from_combinatorics_to_mass_spectrum.tex}.

\section*{Current Lean build split}
The repository exposes the following \emph{named} Lake targets (see \path{lakefile.toml} for the authoritative list):
\begin{itemize}
\item \texttt{lake build HQIVPaperClaims}: lightweight manuscript target using the symbolic interface module \path{Hqiv/SO8ClosureSymbolic.lean} (no heavy matrix-certificate elaboration).
\item \texttt{lake build HQIVSO8Closure}: full certified matrix-closure target (\path{Hqiv/GeneratorsLieClosureData*.lean}, \path{Hqiv/GeneratorsLieClosure.lean}, \path{Hqiv/Algebra/SO8ClosureAbstract.lean}).
